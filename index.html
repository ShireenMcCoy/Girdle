Welcome to Girdle, another Wordle knockoff.

The secret word wraps clockwise in a circle.

The word can start at any point in the circle, but you must start entering letters from the top square.

GREEN means the letter is in the right spot.

YELLOW means the letter is in the word, but is in the wrong spot.

GRAY means the letter is not in the word.

Entering non-valid words is allowed.
